# 🎣 Phishing Awareness Training

## 📌 Overview

This project provides a comprehensive awareness module on phishing attacks, including identification techniques, prevention strategies, and real-world examples.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Understand what phishing attacks are
* Identify phishing emails and fake websites
* Learn social engineering tactics
* Apply best practices to stay safe

---

## ⚠️ What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, credit card details, or personal data.

---

## 🔍 How to Recognize Phishing

### 📧 Suspicious Emails

* Unknown sender
* Urgent or threatening language
* Spelling/grammar mistakes
* Suspicious attachments or links

### 🌐 Fake Websites

* Misspelled URLs
* No HTTPS (🔒)
* Poor design or copied layout

---

## 🧠 Social Engineering Techniques

* Impersonation (banks, companies)
* Urgency ("Your account will be locked!")
* Fear tactics
* Curiosity bait ("You won a prize!")

---

## 🛡️ Best Practices

* Never click unknown links
* Verify sender identity
* Use 2FA (Two-Factor Authentication)
* Keep software updated
* Use antivirus/security tools
